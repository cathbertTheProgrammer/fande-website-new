F & E Enterprises Limited

Who Are We?

F & E Enterprises Limited is an incorporated Company under the Companies Act Cap 388 of the Laws of Zambia. It was incorporated as a Company limited by shares on 22nd September 2021 (Business Registration No.120210021003).

It is governed by the organisation’s articles of associations, the Companies Act, and other related laws relating to the business.

The Company has registered a subsidiary consultant subsidiary on 28th September 2021 with Trade Name of F & E Consultants.

The company is tax registered with the Zambia Revenue Authority (ZRA) with Tax Payer Identification Number: 2801377406 and shall ensure that it is tax compliant all the time.
